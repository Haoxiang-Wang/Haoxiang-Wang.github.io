I'm a first-year ECE Ph.D. student from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), working on machine learning with theoretical guarantees under the supervision of Prof. [Bo Li](https://aisecure.github.io/). Previously, I obtained my B.S. from UIUC with double majors in (1) Statistics & Computer Science, (2) Physics. My current research interest is to bridge deep learning theory and applications. Specifically, I'm interested in using deep learning theory (i.e., theory of deep neural networks) to provide theoretical guarantees for some deep learning applications (e.g., meta-learning, adversarial machine learning) or developing application algorithms motivated by deep learning theory.
