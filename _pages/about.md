I am a research scientist at [Luma AI](https://lumalabs.ai/), working on multimodal reasoning and generation. Previously, I was a research scientist at [NVIDIA Research](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/research/), where I was a core contributor to [NVIDIA Cosmos](https://github.com/nvidia/cosmos). I obtained my Ph.D. in 2024 from the [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu/) (UIUC), and my B.S. in 2019 from UIUC with double majors in Physics and Statistics & Computer Science.

My research focuses on post-training for multimodal models and LLMs, including reasoning, RLHF, reward modeling, and synthetic data curation.

- Core contributor to [Uni-1](https://lumalabs.ai/uni-1/tech-specs)
- #1 Elo rating in human preference evaluation across leading image generation models

**[NVIDIA Cosmos](https://github.com/nvidia/cosmos)** — Open models for physical AI.
- Core contributor to [Cosmos-Reason1](https://research.nvidia.com/labs/dir/cosmos-reason1/) (2M+ total [model downloads](https://huggingface.co/nvidia/Cosmos-Reason1-7B)) and [Cosmos-Reason2](https://huggingface.co/nvidia/Cosmos-Reason2-8B), reasoning vision-language models for Physical AI
- Core contributor of [Cosmos World Foundation Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.03575) (8K+ [GitHub Stars](https://github.com/nvidia/cosmos))

**[RLHFlow](https://github.com/RLHFlow)** — Open-source RLHF for LLM post-training.
- Developed [ArmoRM](https://huggingface.co/RLHFlow/ArmoRM-Llama3-8B-v0.1), a multi-objective reward model that achieved #1 on [RewardBench](https://huggingface.co/spaces/allenai/reward-bench) (May 2024), surpassing GPT-4 judges; 400K+ downloads on HuggingFace
- Built the first open-source [online iterative DPO framework](https://github.com/RLHFlow/Online-RLHF)
